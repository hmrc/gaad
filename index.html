Virtual sessions

Lost in paradise - an accessibility testing mystery

Learn how to run a basic accessibility check by solving a mystery.

A shipment risks being lost at a port halfway across the world - it’s up to you to discover what’s being shipped, where it’s from and who needs it. The details of the shipment are found by running an accessibility check on a fictional customs service.

In this one hour session you will:

  • learn how to identify accessibility issues and why it’s important
  • search for accessibility issues using features on your device
  • review your findings facility in our Microsoft Office 365 products

Thursday 16 May, 10am to 11am. To sign up for this event, please fill in the registration form.

DIAS and the evolution of accessibility at HMRC

Learn about the DIAS team and the evolving challenges of accessibility in HMRC. We’ll talk about the team’s history, what’s on the horizon and how we can help.

Thursday 16 May, 2:30pm to 3:30pm. To sign up for this event, please fill in the registration form.
